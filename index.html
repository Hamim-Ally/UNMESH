Founding & Vision

The journey of UNMESH began in 2003,
born from the visionary ideals of Mr. AKM Ashraful Alam,
a former UNICEF officer

His pioneering concept focused on transforming developing countries such as Bangladesh and Tanzania into sustainable societies by leveraging their natural assets — wind, water, solar energy, soil, and above all, people.
