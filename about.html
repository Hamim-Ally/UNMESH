OUR STORY

UNMESH Consulting: Pioneering Sustainable Environmental Practices For Tomorrow’s Challenges

History of UNMESH Consulting

We leverage cutting-edge research and innovative techniques to provide our clients with impactful environmental solutions. Our expertise in waste management and sustainable practices has enabled businesses and communities to implement effective strategies that foster both economic and ecological resilience.

UNMESH Consulting is a Canada-based environmental consultancy and trading company, established with a global vision to bring forward sustainable and environmentally sound solutions. Rooted in the principles of environmental conservation and aligned with both Islamic values of stewardship and the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), UNMESH works to promote ecological awareness and deliver cutting-edge sustainability services to both the public and industrial sectors.

Founding and Vision

Our Commitment to Sustainability and Change

The journey of UNMESH began in 2003, born from the visionary ideals of Mr. AKM Ashraf ul Alam, a former UNICEF officer. His pioneering concept focused on transforming developing countries such as Bangladesh and Tanzania into sustainable societies by leveraging their natural assets—wind, water, solar energy, soil, and above all, people.

 Mr. Alam’s approach was ahead of its time, emerging during the early discourse around the three pillars of sustainability: environmental protection, social equity, and economic viability. His ambition was to create holistic solutions that not only addressed environmental challenges but also empowered communities and stimulated economic growth.

Our Journey

The Evolution of UNMESH Consulting

At UNMESH Consulting, we are committed to creating a cleaner, greener future through innovative environmental, sustainability, and waste management solutions. With a multidisciplinary team of experts, we partner with businesses, governments, and communities to reduce environmental impact, optimize resource use, and implement responsible waste strategies. In addition to environmental solutions, UNMESH also focuses development of the health and education sectors around the globe. It has successfully completed many projects over the last decade with various NGOs and industry partners.

Our mission is to drive meaningful change by integrating sustainable practices into everyday operations, helping our clients meet regulatory requirements, achieve sustainability goals, and make a positive impact on the planet. From environmental assessments and compliance to zero-waste planning and circular economy strategies, we deliver practical, data-driven solutions that make sustainability achievable and measurable.

Together, we can build a more sustainable tomorrow.
